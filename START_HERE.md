## Vault Purpose

This Obsidian vault is the central knowledge base for long-term reusable knowledge.

It is designed to support:

- Quant Finance
- Trading Strategy
- Resume and Career
- Software Projects
- Agent Workflow
- Personal Knowledge Management

---

## Main Workflow

When adding new material:

1. Put raw material into `00_Inbox/`.
2. Open the note in Obsidian.
3. Run Copilot command: `KB - Process Active Note`.
4. Review the generated Intake Report.
5. Create a Literature Note in `02_Literature Notes/`.
6. Extract 1-3 Atomic Notes into `03_Atomic Notes/`.
7. Add reviewed notes to the relevant MOC in `04_MOC/`.
8. Change status from `needs_review` to `evergreen` only after manual review.

---

## Key Folders

- `00_Inbox/`: raw and unprocessed materials
- `01_Sources/`: source notes and original references
- `02_Literature Notes/`: faithful source-based notes
- `03_Atomic Notes/`: reusable single-concept notes
- `04_MOC/`: Maps of Content
- `05_Projects/`: project-specific knowledge and outputs
- `06_Prompts/`: agent prompts and knowledge-base rules
- `_Templates/`: reusable Obsidian note templates
- `_agent/`: operating rules and routing logic
